Based near Bridgwater and covering Somerset and Devon areas.

I'm a high standard, professional face and body artist and have owned my business for over ten years. I love to create smiles, laughter and memories. I am a huge draw for all ages and provide fun, excitement and wonderful photo and social media opportunities. I also offer a wide range of glitter tattoos and have plenty of festival style glitters for the adults. As a professional, you can be assured that you will be getting amazing art work as well as high standard hygiene. I'm friendly, great with all ages and, RELIABLE. I have many designs for guests to choose from, so they will be spoilt for choice! They will be thrilled when they transform into their chosen characters.

When working outdoors I paint from my wonderful, camper van with awning, which both the children and adults love. I just need space to set up in and you can leave the rest to me!
