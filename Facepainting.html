Face Painting

GALLERY

Painting can be on faces, arms, legs, shoulders, décolleté and backs.

Face painting is fantastic for any event where a touch of fun, colour and sparkle add to the entertainment.

Why not consider it for your Hen do, wedding reception or Christening as well as all age parties?

Private bookings are also taken for fancy dress, corporate advertising, Halloween and pregnancy bump painting.
