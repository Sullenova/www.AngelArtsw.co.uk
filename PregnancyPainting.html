What is pregnancy bump painting?

Pregnancy bump painting is a beautiful keepsake of this time in your life. To put in your baby book, to print and frame or even to enlarge as a canvas.

Any ideas you have on colours theme or images, potential baby names or your baby's due date, can all be incorporated to design a bespoke piece of artwork that is unique to you, painted onto your bump and then photographed.

Partners or other children can be included in the photos and even have face paints to match!

Sittings average an hour and a half dependant on detail required.

Only EU cosmetic paints are used and are easily washed off with soap and water.

Just sit back, relax and enjoy the experience!
